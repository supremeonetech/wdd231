MoneyMap - Personal Expense Tracker

Site Name

MoneyMap was chosen because it represents a way to "map" and visualize personal financial activities. The name is short, memorable, and relevant to budgeting and financial management.

Optional domain: moneymaptracker.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of MoneyMap is to help users track daily expenses, organize spending by category, and generate visual reports such as monthly summaries and charts. This tool empowers users to stay in control of their finances and make informed decisions.

Scenarios

  • How can I record my daily expenses quickly from my phone?
  • How much have I spent this month on groceries?
  • Can I view a pie chart of my expenses by category?
